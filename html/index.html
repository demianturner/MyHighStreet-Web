My High Street

Available on the iPhone

My High Street is a new app for the iPhone that helps UK residents locate their favourite brands and retailers based on current location. Finding the nearest Boots or H+M between meetings has never been easier!

Simply use the iPhone to select your favourite retailer from a list of categories, click the locate button and you will be given a map with directions straight to the closest branch near you.

Features

  • finds you the retailer's phone number, website and all contact details
  • shows you directions how to get to the shop
  • and lists all similar retailers in your vicinity.
  • you can even use the StreetView feature to preview the location before you arrive.
